[ Psyris ]

Psycheris

Discovered by / at:

Crete, Greece

Family:

Nymphalidae

Habitat:

FMediterranean climates, flower fields

Appearance:

Iridescent or translucent wings with a small round body

Key Characteristics:

  • Adapts by changing color based on interaction with the environment
  • Leaves bioluminescent trails
  • Exhibit friendly behavior towards humans

What we know so far about Psyris:The Psyris are passive creatures found commonly in Crete flying in pairs. Due to their bioluminescent trails that can easily attract predators, they developed an adaptive coloration allowing them to blend in with their environment.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature: Their friendly nature makes it easy for humans to approach and they respond in fits of changing colors. They will also follow you if they are feed pollen but will fly away once they finish eating it.
